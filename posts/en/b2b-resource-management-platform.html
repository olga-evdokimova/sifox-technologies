B2B

B2B

RMP is a unified B2B solution that streamlines mobile data and voice management for companies and organizations. Combining three powerful capabilities in a single system with one database and user-friendly interface, RMP empowers businesses to efficiently control mobile resources.

Data and Voice Distribution:

Easily distribute mobile data and voice to end-users. Ideal for companies needing to resell or allocate resources for employees, with flexibility in managing usage.

Gifting for Campaigns:

Supports marketing and engagement efforts by enabling companies to gift data or voice packages during promotional campaigns, building stronger customer or employee engagement.

Reverse Data Billing:

Enables free access to specific websites or mobile apps by covering data costs, ensuring seamless engagement with designated online resources at no cost to the end-user.
