Buy for Me is an innovative feature designed to empower subscribers of the same telecom operator to support each other by sharing mobile data or voice packages. It offers a convenient, user-friendly way for one subscriber (the Requestor) to request a package from another (the Sponsor), fostering connection and collaboration.

The feature enables seamless mobile data and voice sharing within families and friends. The Requestor (User A) can easily request data or voice from a Sponsor (User B), who decides the amount to provide, completes the payment, and sends it directly to the Requestor. This feature, available within the same mobile network operator (MNO), offers flexibility in staying connected and making data or voice more affordable.

Benefits for Users
Affordability:

Allows users to share data and voice packages, reducing costs and increasing accessibility for family and friends.

Convenience:

Simple request and approval process through the app, making it easy to stay connected.

Flexibility:

The Sponsor controls the amount of data or voice sent, making it customizable to individual needs.

Benefits for Telecom Operators
Increased Usage:

Drives higher data and voice consumption within the network.

Enhanced Customer Retention:

Promotes engagement and strengthens customer loyalty.

Revenue Growth:

Encourages additional purchases and keeps spending within the MNO, driving up ARPU (Average Revenue Per User).
