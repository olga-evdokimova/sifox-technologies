The feature allows subscribers to stay connected even when they have zero airtime balance. With this feature, a caller with insufficient balance (the Requestor) can still make a call by prompting the receiver (the Sponsor) to cover the cost. This feature offers an easy way for friends and family to stay in touch, even in times of low balance.

Benefits for Users
Continued Connectivity:

Ensures users can reach out even when they lack airtime.

Convenience:

Simple process to initiate a call with zero balance, making it easy to stay connected in critical moments

Supportive for Families and Friends:

Allows loved ones to assist each other effortlessly.

Benefits for Telecom Operators
Increased Call Activity:

Maintains call volume by allowing more calls to be completed.

Enhanced User Loyalty:

Strengthens relationships with customers who value flexible options for staying connected.

Revenue Growth:

Enables more call completions and airtime spending within the network, boosting ARPU (Average Revenue Per User).
