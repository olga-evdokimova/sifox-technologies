PRODUCTS

The specialized data plans or packages that allow subscribers to access certain online services at reduced data usage costs. These plans are designed to cater to the increasing demand for data usage on popular social media, messaging apps and video hosting platforms.

Social Data Bundles:

Mobile operators collaborate with popular messaging apps (like WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger), social networks (like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter), and video hosting platforms (like YouTube, Netflix) to create specialized data bundles.

Benefits for Subscribers:

Subscribers benefit from these bundles by being able to use their favorite messaging apps, stay active on social media, or watch videos without worrying about data overage charges. It encourages users to engage more with these platforms on their mobile devices.

Time-Limited or Unlimited Access:

Some bundles may provide unlimited access to the designated apps or services during a specified time period, such as a day, week, or month. Others may have data caps or limits.

The social messaging, networkding or media bundles are a win-win for both mobile operators and consumers. Operators can attract and retain customers by providing value-added services, while consumers get to enjoy their favorite online activities without worrying about excessive data costs.
