PRODUCTS

A convenient and cost-effective option for households

Cost Savings:

Shared family voice and data packages typically offer discounts compared to individual plans. With shared data and voice minutes, families can avoid the expenses associated with multiple separate plans

Shared Data Pool:

Family sponsor can share a single pool of data, making it easier to manage data usage collectively. This reduces the risk of overage charges and ensures that everyone has access to data when they need it.

Limitless calling within the family:

Voice calls becomes much cheaper between family members. This eliminates concerns about exceeding voice minute limits and encourages regular communication within the family.

Simplified billing and account nanbagement:

With a shared package family sponsor easily makes an order and adds the family members via web interface or USSD menu, controlling fund and data sufficiency.

Overall, mobile data and voice sharing for families provide a cost-effective solution, fosters better communication among family members and streamlines communication budget handling to meet the specific needs of each relative.
